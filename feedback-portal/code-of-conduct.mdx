We ask all users to adhere to the following code of conduct to maintain a respectful and collaborative community. By participating in the forum, you agree to comply with the guidelines below.

### Be Respectful

Treat all forum members with courtesy and respect. Disagreements and debates are natural, but personal attacks, offensive language, or harassment will not be tolerated.

### Stay on Topic

Keep discussions relevant to the forum's purpose. Avoid derailing threads with unrelated content. If you have a new topic to discuss, start a new thread.

### No Spam or Self-Promotion

Refrain from posting promotional content or spam. This includes advertising products, services, or websites unrelated to the forum's focus.

### Be Constructive

Provide feedback and criticism in a constructive manner. Aim to contribute positively to discussions and help fellow users.

### Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others. Respect the privacy of forum members and refrain from posting sensitive or confidential information.

### No Discrimination

Do not engage in any form of discrimination, including but not limited to race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or disability. This forum is an inclusive space for everyone.

### No Illegal Content

Do not share or promote any illegal content, including but not limited to pirated software, malware, or any form of intellectual property infringement.

### Report Inappropriate Content

If you come across any content that violates this code of conduct, report it promptly to [support@omnisets.com](mailto:support@omnisets.com). Do not engage in public arguments over rule violations.

### Respect Moderators

Follow the instructions of forum moderators.

### No Multiple Accounts

Each user is allowed only one account. Creating multiple accounts may result in a ban.

### Stay Informed

Keep yourself informed about any updates or changes to the forum's policies. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violations.

Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent banning from the forum, depending on the severity and frequency of the infractions. The OmniSets team reserves the right to enforce these rules at their discretion.
