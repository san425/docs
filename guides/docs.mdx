Docs allow you to create, organize, and attach documents directly to your study sets or use them as standalone resources. This guide will walk you through the key functionalities and how to make the most of this feature.

### Creating a Doc
1. Head over to the `Docs` tab in the dashboard.
2. Click on the "+" button to create a new doc.
