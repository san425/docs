<Info>
You can only export if you are logged in to your account!
</Info>


## Exporting as text
1. Head over to your studyset by clicking on it in the dashboard.
2. Click on the `Export` button on the bottom right corner of the page. It's the one with an arrow pointing out of the file!
<Frame>
    <img width="500px" src="/images/export-button.png" alt="Export button" />
</Frame>
3. You'll be prompted to choose your delimiters (essentially, what goes in between cards and the term/definitions.
<Info>
    We recommend using the default delimiters (hashtags), but if you're importing from another app, you may want to change them to match the format of that app.
</Info>
4. Choose your delimiters!
<Frame>
    <img width="500px" src="/images/export-modal-delimiters.png" alt="Delimiters" />
</Frame>
5. Select all the text in the box and copy it.
6. That's it! You can now paste your flashcards wherever you'd like. Including your favorite flashcard app (which is hopefully OmniSets ðŸ˜‰).

## Exporting as a CSV
1. Head over to your studyset by clicking on it in the dashboard.
2. Click on the `Export` button on the bottom right corner of the page. It's the one with an arrow pointing out of the file!
<Frame>
    <img width="500px" src="/images/export-button.png" alt="Export button" />
</Frame>
3. Click on the `Export as CSV` button.
<Frame>
    <img width="500px" src="/images/export-csv-button.png" alt="Export as CSV button" />
</Frame>
4. That's it! Your CSV file will be downloaded to your device. You can now open it in your favorite spreadsheet program (like Excel or Google Sheets).
